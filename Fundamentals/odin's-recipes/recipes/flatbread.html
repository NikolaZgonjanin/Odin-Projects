Flatbread

The most simple bread made with only a few ingredients that can go along with most dishes or eaten just by itself.

Ingredients:

  • 250g Flour
  • 250ml Water
  • 1 tsp Salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour and the salt in a larg bowl.
  2. Gradually add the water, stirring until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough until the dough is smooth.
  4. Seperate the dough into 6-8 pieces and roll each peace into a thin, round flatbread.
  5. Heat dry frying pan over medium heat and cook each flatbread for 2-3 minutes on each side.

Enjoy!
