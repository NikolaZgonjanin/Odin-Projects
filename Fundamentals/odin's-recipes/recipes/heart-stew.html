Hearty Stew

A staple in the Viking diet, this rich and flavourful stew is made with slow-cooked meats, root vegetables, and a mix of spices. This recipe is perfect for a cold winter night or a filling meal after a long day of raiding.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Beef, cubed
  • 2 Onions, chopped
  • 3 Carrots, chopped
  • 2 Parsnips, chopped
  • 2 Turnips, chopped
  • 1l Beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Dried thyme
  • 1 tsp Dried sage
  • 1 tsp Dried rosemary

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until softened.
  2. Add the meat to cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the carrots parsnips, turnips, salt, pepper, thyme, sage, and rosemary.
  4. Pour in the beef broth, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the meat is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with a side of flatbread or rye bread.

Enjoy!
